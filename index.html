NARUTO CHARACTERS

Team 7

Hatake Kakashi
Shinobi of Konoha and 6th hokage of village
Famed as Kakashi of the Sharingan , he is one of Konoha's most talented ninja, regularly looked to for advice and leadership despite his personal dislike of responsibility. After the Fourth Shinobi World War, Kakashi becomes Konoha's Sixth Hokage
Uzumaki Naruto
Shinobi of Konoha
Naruto Uzumaki (うずまきナルト, Uzumaki Naruto) is a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uzumaki clan. He became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of his birth — a fate that caused him to be shunned by most of Konoha throughout his childhood.
Uchiha Sasuke
Shinobi of Konoha
Sasuke belongs to the Uchiha clan, a notorious ninja family, and one of the most powerful, allied with Konohagakure . Most of its members were massacred by Sasuke's older brother, Itachi Uchiha, leaving Sasuke one of the few living.Sasuke made it his mission in life to avenge them by killing Itachi.
Haruno Sakura
Medical Ninja of Konoha
Sakura Haruno is a kunoichi of Konohagakure. When assigned to Team 7, Sakura quickly finds herself ill-prepared for the duties of a shinobi. However, after training under the Sannin Tsunade, she overcomes this, and becomes recognised as one of the greatest medical-nin in the world.
